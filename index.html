ABOUT

My Journey Into Cybersecurity

My fascination with cybersecurity began in middle school when I first encountered the concept of ethical hacking. What started as curiosity about how systems work quickly evolved into a deep passion for understanding their vulnerabilities and building defenses against malicious attacks.

Through countless hours of self-study, hands-on practice, and certification pursuits, I've developed expertise in penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and security research. My approach combines technical proficiency with ethical responsibility, always focusing on improving security postures rather than exploiting weaknesses.

My ultimate dream is to join India's Research and Analysis Wing (RAW), where I can leverage my cybersecurity skills to protect national interests and contribute to the country's digital security infrastructure. Every project I undertake and every skill I develop brings me closer to this goal.
